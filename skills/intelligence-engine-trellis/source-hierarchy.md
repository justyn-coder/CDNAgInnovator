# Trellis Domain: Source Hierarchy

## Purpose
Authoritative source order for Trellis research (Canadian agtech ecosystem: people, programs, organizations, events). Check these in order. Higher-ranked sources override lower-ranked sources when data conflicts.

**Status:** Draft. Will be refined during calibration runs.

---

## Government / Regulatory (Authoritative)

1. **AAFC (Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada)** - aafc-aac.gc.ca - Program data, funding announcements, policy documents, research station publications, Canadian Agricultural Partnership details
2. **Innovation Canada** - innovation.canada.ca - Federal funding programs, business support directory, SR&ED eligibility
3. **Provincial agriculture ministries:**
   - Ontario: OMAFRA (omafra.gov.on.ca) - Programs, grants, research, Greenbelt initiatives
   - Alberta: Alberta Agriculture (alberta.ca/agriculture) - Programs, irrigation districts, ag innovation
   - Saskatchewan: Ministry of Agriculture (saskatchewan.ca/agriculture) - Crop insurance, research, APAS
   - BC: Ministry of Agriculture (gov.bc.ca/agriculture) - Investment programs, food processing
   - Manitoba: Manitoba Agriculture (gov.mb.ca/agriculture) - Programs, EMILI partnership
   - Quebec: MAPAQ - Ag programs, innovation grants
4. **Statistics Canada** - statcan.gc.ca - Census of Agriculture, farm financial data, ag trade stats

## Programs / Accelerators (Semi-Authoritative)

5. **Bioenterprise Canada** - bioenterprise.ca - Guelph-based ag innovation hub. Program details, cohort listings, event calendars
6. **Foresight Canada** - foresightcac.com - Cleantech/agtech accelerator. Venture and cohort details
7. **Creative Destruction Lab (CDL)** - creativedestructionlab.com - Ag stream cohort listings
8. **MaRS Discovery District** - marsdd.com - Ag/food ventures, market intelligence reports
9. **Protein Industries Canada** - proteinindustries.ca - Supercluster investments, co-investment projects
10. **EMILI (Enterprise Machine Intelligence & Learning Initiative)** - emilicanada.com - Manitoba ag-AI hub
