# Trellis Domain: Query Library

## Purpose
Domain-specific search patterns for Canadian agtech ecosystem research. Organized by entity type.

**Status:** Draft. Will be expanded during calibration runs.

---

## Person Research

### Finding First-Person Content (Podcasts, Presentations, Interviews)
- `"[person name]" agriculture podcast interview`
- `"[person name]" conference presentation speaker agtech`
- `"[person name]" webinar agriculture innovation`
- `"[person name]" RealAgriculture OR "Real Agriculture"`
- `"[person name]" Bioenterprise OR Foresight OR CDL`
- `"[person name]" AAFC OR OMAFRA OR "agriculture canada"`
- `"[person name]" agtech keynote OR panel OR fireside`
- `"[person name]" site:youtube.com agriculture` (title/description only per U1)
- `"[person name]" transcript podcast agriculture`

### Verifying Affiliation and Role
- `"[person name]" "[organization]" site:linkedin.com`
- `"[person name]" "[organization]" announcement OR appointed OR joins`
- `"[person name]" "[organization]" conference speaker bio`

### Finding Expertise and Perspective
- `"[person name]" precision agriculture OR agtech OR farm technology`
- `"[person name]" quote OR interview OR opinion agriculture`
- `"[person name]" board OR advisory OR committee agriculture`

## Organization Research

### Program and Accelerator Discovery
- `"[org name]" agtech program cohort`
- `"[org name]" agriculture innovation funding`
- `"[org name]" farm technology adoption case study`
- `"[org name]" accelerator incubator agriculture canada`
- `"[org name]" portfolio companies agriculture`
- `"[org name]" annual report [current year]`

### Partnerships and Ecosystem Position
- `"[org name]" partner OR partnership agriculture`
- `"[org name]" AAFC OR OMAFRA OR "Innovation Canada"`
- `"[org name]" Bioenterprise OR "Protein Industries" OR EMILI`
- `"[org name]" investment OR co-investment agtech`

### Activity and Signals
- `"[org name]" [current year] announcement OR launch OR funding`
- `"[org name]" hiring agriculture OR agtech`
- `"[org name]" expansion OR new program OR new partnership`

## Program Research

### Status and Eligibility
- `"[program name]" intake deadline application [current year]`
- `"[program name]" eligibility criteria`
- `"[program name]" alumni cohort companies`
- `"[program name]" funding amount maximum`
- `"[program name]" closed OR "no longer accepting" OR expired`
- `site:[government domain] "[program name]"`

### Impact and Results
- `"[program name]" results OR outcomes OR impact report`
- `"[program name]" companies funded OR supported`
- `"[program name]" success stories OR case studies`

- `"[event name]" [year] presentation slides filetype:pdf`

## Cross-Cutting Queries

### Mandatory Recency Search (CP12)
- `"[entity name]" [current year]` - ALWAYS run first
- `"[entity name]" announcement OR news [current year]`

### Counter-Evidence Search (VP2)
- `"[entity name]" award OR recognition agriculture`
- `"[entity name]" innovation OR best practice`
- `"[entity name]" criticism OR controversy OR challenge`

### Association Directory Search (CP9)
- `"[entity name]" member OR membership agtech OR agriculture association`
- `"[entity name]" site:bioenterprise.ca OR site:foresightcac.com`
- `"[entity name]" directory agriculture canada`
