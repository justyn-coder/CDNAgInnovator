# Trellis Brand Guide

## Name
**Trellis**
Tagline: Navigate Canada's AgTech Ecosystem

## Logo Concept
A trellis grid (3 vertical supports × 3 crossbars) with growth dots climbing upward. The trellis structure fades away at the top — verticals stop short, no top crossbar — while dots continue floating into open space. The dots transition from green (bottom) through chartreuse (middle) to harvest gold (top), representing growth from planting through to harvest/return.

## Color Palette

| Name          | Hex       | Usage                              |
|---------------|-----------|-------------------------------------|
| Deep forest   | `#1B4332` | Dark backgrounds, app icon bg, text |
| Trellis green | `#2D7A4F` | Trellis structure strokes           |
| Growth        | `#48B87A` | Bottom dots, primary green accent   |
| Chartreuse    | `#8CC63F` | Middle dots, transition green       |
| Harvest gold  | `#D4A828` | Top/floating dots, accent, CTA      |
| Warm light    | `#F5E6B8` | Light backgrounds, subtle fills     |

## Typography

- **Wordmark/Display:** DM Serif Display (400 weight)
- **Body/Tagline/UI:** DM Sans (400, 500 weights)
- Tagline is uppercase with 0.05em letter-spacing

## Logo Rules
- The icon always shows the trellis dissolving at the top with dots floating free
- Minimum clear space around the logo = half the icon height
- On dark backgrounds: trellis structure is white, dots keep brand colors
- On dark backgrounds: tagline uses harvest gold (#D4A828)
- On light backgrounds: trellis structure uses deep forest (#1B4332)
